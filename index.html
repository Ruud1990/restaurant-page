about us

Take your tastebuds on an international trip at The Food Dream, a new exotic restaurant and cocktail bar that aims to surprise and inspire patrons with a unique rotating monthly menu that highlights different countries’ cultures.

Inspired by the city’s rich diversity and vibrant mix of world influences, Founder, Anna Nowak, designed the restaurant to take diners on a tasting tour across the globe. An acclaimed East Europe Restaurateur, Mihajlov, has worked in the industry for over eight years opening notable Warsaw establishments such as Olio e Piu, Dominique’s Bistro, Whynot Coffee and Boucherie.
